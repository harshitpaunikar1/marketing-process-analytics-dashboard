# Marketing Process Analytics Dashboard

> **Domain:** Marketing / AdTech

## Overview

A growing marketing team needed to orchestrate and measure multi-step campaigns end-to-end. Manual email handoffs, inconsistent tracking of opens, clicks, and replies, and scattered data made daily performance unclear. Leaders lacked simple views of start-of-day and end-of-day process health and could not link activity to outcomes across customer segments. Without automation and an analytical dashboard, the team risked wasted spend, slow follow-ups, and missed revenue because issues surfaced late. The objective: centralise activity data, automate repeatable journeys, and provide at-a-glance views of DAU/MAU trends, process throughput, and segment value so marketing could act quickly and confidently.

## Approach

- Ran discovery with marketing and ops to map workflows, define EOD/SOD KPIs, align on DAU/MAU and engagement metrics
- Unified email activity data (opens, clicks, replies, bounces) and built governed data pipeline with ID stitching and data quality checks
- Automated email flows using rules and triggers; logged every step to enable auditability and time-to-action measurement
- Implemented analytics layers: RFM segmentation per cohort, process throughput/latency views, conversion funnels, anomaly alerts
- Delivered role-based dashboard with drill-downs, self-serve filters, scheduled EOD/SOD summaries to stakeholders
- Operated in iterative sprints with UAT, documentation, enablement sessions for smooth adoption
